Project Construction Features

Admin Panel:

  • Dashboard (Contact Table): Provides an overview of recent contact form submissions for quick access and response.
  • Add Constructed Project: Allows administrators to input details of newly completed projects into the system for record-keeping.
  • Show Constructed Project Table (Delete): Displays a table of all constructed projects for reference and management, with the ability to delete outdated or irrelevant entries.
  • Add Services: Enables administrators to add new services offered by the construction company for potential clients to view.
  • Show Services Table (Delete): Displays a table of all services offered, with the option to remove or update service listings as needed.
  • Change Password: Provides the functionality for administrators to update their login credentials securely.
  • Reset Password: Allows administrators to reset their password in case of forgetting or compromising their current credentials.
  • Logout: Securely logs out administrators from the system to prevent unauthorized access.

User Interface:

  • Home: Introduces the construction company and its services to visitors.
  • About: Provides information about the construction company, its history, mission, and vision.
  • Projects (Completed): Showcases a gallery or list of completed construction projects to demonstrate the company's expertise and capabilities.
  • Services: Presents an overview of the services offered by the construction company, including descriptions and possibly pricing information.
  • Submit Contact Form: Allows visitors to submit inquiries or requests for information or services by filling out a contact form.
  • Login Admin: Grants access to the admin panel for authorized personnel to manage projects, services, and contact submissions.
